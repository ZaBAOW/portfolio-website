About me

Hi, I'm Andrew Kadesky. I'm a fullstack developer and a recent graduate of the Fullstack bootcamp at Thinkful. Problem solving has always been something I find impossible to ignore even in subjects I'm not as well versed in. I started in the field of life sciences, but was drawn to the computer sciences.

I spend a lot of time on a computer and more specifically on the internet. When I am away from the keyboard, I spend a good amount of time up in the snow covered mountains snowboarding and among the chatter of a brew pub tasting and enjoying the local and, when available, international drafts.
